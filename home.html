Welcome to Digos City

Digos City is a first-class city and the capital of the province of Davao del Sur, Philippines. It is located in the southeastern part of Mindanao, known for its natural beauty and vibrant culture. Digos is a growing urban center with various attractions including parks, historical sites, and delicious local cuisine.

Whether you're interested in exploring nature, experiencing local festivals, or simply enjoying the warmth of Filipino hospitality, Digos has something to offer for everyone.
